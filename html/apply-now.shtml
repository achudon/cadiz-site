HOW DO I APPLY TO THE PROGRAM?

In order to apply to the program, you must complete the following paperwork:

Departmental Application (pdf)

Requires basic information, as well as the names of 2 college-level Spanish professors who will recommend you to the program.

IPE Application (IPE site)

Requires you to respond to some short answer questions and write one essay on why you want to be part of the program.

BOTH of these forms are required in order for you to be considered for the program.

APPLICATION DEADLINES:

Fall Semester: March 20

Spring Semester: October 20

Generally all qualified candidates who complete the application on time are accepted.
