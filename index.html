Live and study in Cádiz, Spain, for a semester or a full academic year.

Live in Cádiz, Spain

Students in the NW Cádiz program live with local host families close the university for the duration of their stay.

Attend the University of Cádiz

Take classes taught in Spanish at the University of Cádiz's Facultad de Filosofía y Letras.

Experience Spain and Europe

Use Cádiz as a base to explore more of Europe, as well as Morocco and the Canary Islands.
